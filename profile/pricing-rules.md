# Pricing Rules

## Default posture
- Do not race to the bottom
- Frame pricing around commercial impact and scope complexity
- Prefer senior-operator positioning over commodity pricing

## Hourly guidance
- Base reference rate from existing profile.json: $35/hr
- For clearly senior retention/lifecycle strategy work, present pricing as scope-dependent and avoid sounding rigid
- If the client posted a realistic hourly range, anchor within that range when possible

## Fixed-price guidance
- If the work is an audit, first-phase plan, or lifecycle rebuild, suggest scoping around the first high-impact fixes before proposing a long rollout
- Avoid promising a huge scope inside a low fixed budget

## Connects posture
- Use connects conservatively
- Boost only when the opportunity quality is strong and the budget/fit justify it
- Sensitive or weak-fit roles should not trigger aggressive attach-or-bid behavior
