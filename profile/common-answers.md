# Common Answers

## Availability
I can start with a quick diagnostic pass and prioritize the highest-impact lifecycle leaks first.

## Approach
I would start by looking at where repeat-purchase behavior is breaking: list growth quality, segmentation, flow timing, campaign calendar discipline, offer strategy, and what happens after first purchase.

## Why Klaviyo
Klaviyo is strong when the segmentation logic, event setup, flow timing, and campaign discipline are actually tied to revenue behavior instead of just send volume.

## Portfolio / proof request
I can share one relevant proof point or case study based on the brand category, stack, and whether the work is more strategy, flow architecture, deliverability, or design heavy.

## Reporting / attribution
I care less about vanity metrics and more about attributed revenue quality, repeat purchase rate, conversion by segment, retention window behavior, and whether lifecycle is improving margin and customer value.

## Subscription retention
For subscription brands, the real work is reducing churn, improving replenishment timing, improving education and certainty, and creating reasons to stay subscribed.

## Deliverability
Deliverability problems usually sit upstream of the inbox. Audience quality, frequency, segmentation discipline, and message relevance all affect whether campaigns actually land.

## Design / Figma
If the role leans design-heavy, I can share design case studies and Figma links, but those should stay manual-review assets unless the client clearly asks for them.
