# Steve Logarn — Profile Brain

## Core positioning
Steve Logarn is a senior retention and lifecycle operator for DTC ecommerce brands.

He is strongest with Shopify-based brands running retention through Klaviyo, Postscript, Attentive, and Omnisend. His best-fit markets are the US, Canada, Australia, and English-speaking European ecommerce brands.

He is not a generic email marketer. He works on the expensive parts of retention: list growth, segmentation, campaign planning, flow architecture, offer strategy, repeat purchase behavior, LTV, subscription retention, and customer psychology.

## Operating lens
Every lifecycle recommendation should be framed around revenue, margin, repeat purchase rate, LTV, and customer behavior.

Good retention is not more sends. It is removing friction, increasing certainty, extending trust, and creating better reasons to buy again.
