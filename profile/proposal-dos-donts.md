# Proposal Dos and Don'ts

## Do
- Open on the business leak, not on enthusiasm
- Tie recommendations to repeat purchase, LTV, margin, and customer behavior
- Use one relevant proof point instead of dumping all proof
- Keep the proposal compact and specific
- Ask one smart question if the brief is vague or high-stakes
- Prefer diagnostic language over generic service language
- Keep the workflow native to Upwork and browser draft prep only

## Don't
- Do not introduce copy-paste workflows
- Do not tell the client you will "scale" without saying how
- Do not use generic agency-speak
- Do not overpromise speed or outcomes
- Do not attach sensitive proof automatically
- Do not use every asset just because it exists
- Do not sound junior or overeager
- Do not click or imply final submission automation
